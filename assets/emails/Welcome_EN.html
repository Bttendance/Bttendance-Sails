Welcome to BTTENDANCE!

BTTENDANCE is a mobile application helping to make lecture interactive.

It helps to check students' response in class, to reduce the time spent the attendance check, and to notify important information to students.

Make your lecture effective and efficient with BTTENDANCE!

Clicker

Clicker is the Audience Response System that enables instructors and students to interact dynamically through question-and-polling. See how to use.

Attendance

BTTENDANCE checks your students' attendance accurately and effortlessly in all ranges of class by using Bluetooth and elaborate algorithm. See how to use.

Notice

BTTENDANCE is an efficient way to make an announcement for your class. Notify to your students as easy as sending text message! See how to use.
